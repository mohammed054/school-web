About VISS

“Victoria International School of Sharjah was founded with one purpose to build a space for all students to grow, learn and create with each passing day. VISION

  • The Victoria International School of Sharjah (VISS) is a learning community that supports the attainment of excellence.

MISSION

What VISS OFFERS

At VISS, students embark on a transformational, personal journey, where the combination of ambitious academics, purposeful growth, and active community engagement leads to a futuregreater than you ever thought possible.

Curriculum

Contemporary Pedagogy and Authentic Learning Experiences

Age appropriate pedagogy ensuring personalised learning experiences regardless of your childs grade level. Our pedagogy is grounded in a combination of explicit teaching, supported by guided learning and inquiry approaches to ensure a balanced approach to teaching and learning.

Learning Enhancement

Applying the Response to Intervention model we support class-based personalised learning. Children with specific learning needs as well as the Gifted and Talented student are identified early, provisions implemented and Individualised learning plans developed to address specific needs.

Multiple Curriculum

Contemporary curriculum building the essential foundations preparing your child for multiple career pathways and the ability to access the best local or international universities:

Wellbeing and Values

The five VISS values of Excellence, Diversity, Integrity, Learning and Community, in addition to Wellbeing are at the heart of everything we do. We understand that academic progress and attainment are underpinned by your childs emotional, social and physical needs being an important priority.

Message from PRINCIPAL

Dan OReilly

PRINCIPAL

It is with great pleasure that we welcome you to theVictorian International School Sharjah(VISS Sharjah) – a place where excellence in education meets a welcoming and challenging learning environment. At VISS, we are committed to providing a world-classAustralian educationthat fosters the development of respectful and responsible students.

Our school is a vibrant and inclusive community that values innovation, creativity, and lifelong learning. We believe that every student has the potential to succeed, and our dedicated staff work tirelessly to inspire, challenge, and support them on their educational journey. With a commitment to educating both the heart and mind of each student and combining this with our world class facilities, we know our environment prepares our students to thrive in a rapidly changing world.We encourage activecollaboration between students, parents, and teachers, as we know that education is most powerful when we work together

Whether you are a new family joining us or a long-standing member of theVISS community,we look forward to a future filled withgrowth, learning, and success.We invite you to explore our website and discover the many opportunities that await your child.

Explore our SCHOOL

Early Learning Centre

Welcome to the VISS Early Learning Centre (ELC), a place where memories of laughter, friendships, exploration, joy, and rich learning opportunities begin.

Primary School

Welcome to VISS Primary School, where we empower every student to thrive in a rich academic environment. Our holistic approach, grounded in the Victorian Curriculum, fosters curiosity, creativity, and collaboration while promoting academic excellence and social-emotional growth.

Middle School

Welcome to VISS Middle School, where Grades 6-9 students embark on a transformative journey of intellectual, emotional, and physical growth. Our dynamic learning environment fosters advanced literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking skills through an integrated curriculum, with a focus on English, Humanities, and creative disciplines.

Senior School

Welcome to the Senior School at Victoria International School of Sharjah (VISS). VISS Senior School comprises of Grades 10, 11 and 12. Grade 10 is the final year of the Australian Curriculum and also a preparatory year for the programmes offered in Grade 11 and 12.
