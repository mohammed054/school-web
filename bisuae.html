BTEC Results 2025

We are proud to announce our outstanding BTEC results for 2025, with 77% of our students achieving Distinction grades. Our achievers have secured admissions to top universities locally and globally.
Academics

IGCSE Results 2025

We are proud to celebrate the outstanding achievements of our IGCSE student this year. With 100% pass rate across all subjects, an impressive 82% of grades awarded at A-A*, and 36% of students achieving 6A or more, our learners have truly excelled.
Academics

AS/A Level Outstanding Results

BIS students have once again excelled in their AS/A Level examinations! An impressive 82% of our students achieved A-B*, with several securing multiple A* and A grades across subjects.
Excellence

BSME Membership

We are proud to announce that BIS has recently become a member of British School of the Middle East (BSME) which sets us apart from other school as we join a network of high quality, high performing schools.
