Frontline International School on YouTube

British Curriculum School in Ajman

Frontline International School was established with an ennobling vision: Creating the lifelong learners, by imparting holistic,value based education, in an environment of excellence, where students and faculty from different parts of the world come together for the purpose of learning. We proudly stand as the Best British curriculum school in Ajman. Recognized by the Ministry of Education with a ‘GOOD’ rating, we are honored to be the first school in Ajman to achieve this distinction.

  • International Curriculum
  • Well trained Faculty
  • Community based activities
  • Safe Environment

Principal's Message

It is a great privilege and honour to serve the school community in enabling my aim to establish Frontline School as the best school in Ajman.

Having qualified as a teacher of mathematics at the University of Cambridge in 2003, I bring over two decades of working in schools with more than fifteen years in school leadership both in England and in the UAE. I have been Principal Designate in England as well as having been Principal twice in Dubai previously. My track record of sustained school improvement, growing school leaders and sharing thought leadership as a published author, is evidenced in all the schools that I have worked in. With this, I ensure that I commit to fulfilling the hopes of the students, the aspirations of the parents and the ambitions of the staff.

I am honoured to have an excellent staff community who work tirelessly to ensure the very best for each and every child in our school of more than 1500 students from nearly 50 nationalities. We are, proudly, the fastest growing British curriculum school in Ajman, and now with our first A level cohort, we have the full British curriculum provision from Foundation Stage 1 (Pre-KG), aged 3 years old, up to Year 13 (Grade 12) for young adults aged 18 years old.

Mr Kausor Amin Ali

BEng (Hons.) MA PGCE (Cantab) NPQEL FRSA FCCT
